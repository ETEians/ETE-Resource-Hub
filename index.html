🎓 Admissions
Admission & Program Fees
B.Sc in Electronic & Telecommunication Engineering — a 4-year (8 semester) program. Here's everything you need to know before applying.

Ready to Join ETE at IIUC?

Applications are accepted twice a year — Spring (Jan–Jun) and Autumn (Jul–Dec). Apply online through the official IIUC portal.

🖊️ Apply Online Now 📄 Full Program Details
📋 Admission Requirements
For SSC/HSC Students
Total GPA 6.50 in SSC & HSC combined, minimum GPA 3.00 in each. Physics & Mathematics pass required.
For O/A Level Students
Min. 5 O-Level subjects, 2 A-Level subjects. B grade in 4 subjects, C grade in 3. Physics & Math mandatory.
Program Duration
4 Years / 8 Semesters
Minimum CGPA to Graduate
2.50 CGPA
Admission Test Marks
Physics 25 · Math 30 · Chemistry 15 · English 15 · GK 15 · Viva 10 · GPA 10 = 120
Intake Per Year
Spring + Autumn Semesters
